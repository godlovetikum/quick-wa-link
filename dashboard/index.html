Quick Help

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a wa.me link?

A wa.me link (e.g. wa.me/237650123456) opens a WhatsApp chat to a specific number directly — no need for the recipient to save your number first.

Do I need a WhatsApp account?

No account or login is required to generate a link here. However, the person whose number you're using must have WhatsApp installed and active for the link to open a chat.

Do you store my phone number?

Never. All link generation happens entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your number and message stay private on your device.

Why include a pre-filled message?

Pre-filled messages dramatically reduce the friction of starting a conversation. Customers see exactly what to write, click send, and you get qualified leads instead of blank messages.

Which country should I choose?

Choose the country where your phone number is registered. The country code is the international dialling prefix (e.g. +237 for Cameroon, +44 for UK) added before the local number.

Where can I use my link?

Anywhere: Instagram bio, Facebook posts, TikTok profile, email signatures, business cards, paid ads, website buttons, QR codes — anywhere you want people to start a WhatsApp conversation instantly.
